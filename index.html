About Us

The Orzel Group is a research group dedicated to advancing the field of physics through cutting-edge research in confocal microscopy. Our team is led by Dr. Anna Orzel, a renowned physicist with expertise in confocal microscopy. Our group is comprised of talented researchers, postdocs, and graduate students, who are passionate about pushing the boundaries of this exciting field.

Research

Our research focuses on developing new techniques and methodologies in confocal microscopy, with the aim of improving our understanding of biological systems at the cellular level. Our work has the potential to advance fields such as medicine, biotechnology, and pharmacology.
